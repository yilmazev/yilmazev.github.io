This Month In Habbo: Throwbacks For Spring

A MONTH OF THROWBACKS

For March 2022 in Habbo, we're throwing it back! You'll see the return of some old favourite bundles, collectables, and Diamond Gift Boxes 2.0. Let's get into the details.


We have made a brand new multiple parts bundle for this month that will take you on a riverboat cruise like no other! The Riverboat Cruise Bundle has three parts and each come with their own badge. We love a good badge!

We are also bringing back our Neo-Habbo collectable clothing. First released in 2020, we have now brought them back for 2022 with all-new collectable badges. Collect them all and you will receive an additional Mega Collector badge for completing the set!


The Prime Geode credit furni will also be released this month. She's a pretty one.


DIAMOND GIFT BOXES 2.0

They're back! Some of you might have missed out last year (or you just want to snap up a few more) so here's your chance to get yourself a Diamond Hermes Hat, Diamond Tiara or Diamond Elegant Crown by cracking open one of the Diamond Gift Boxes 2.0. They will be in the shop weekly for a 24 hour period so don't wait too long.
